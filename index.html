About My Dad

Our family loves a good story. We’d spend hours sharing them around a table at our family home. Dad’s stories are the best. He has some of the craziest adventures from his youth I’ve ever heard, and they only get better as he gets older. I sit there in awe, mouth agape like a baby bird as he recounts his life to us. His accounts are always thrilling, hilarious, and action-packed, bordering between fond memory and a tall tale best heard around a bonfire. I want to be like Dad so much and have great stories of my own to tell one day.

I want to be like Dad so much and have great stories of my own to tell one day.

What follows is a collection of some of my favorite memories of my Dad.

Dad grew up in a small village where everyone knew each other. He was a jack-of-all-trades when it came to sports, excelling in cricket, chess, and just about every other game you can think of.

As he got older, he mellowed out from his mischievous school days and became the embodiment of a simple, yet profound, life philosophy. His tales became less about school pranks and more about well, just life! These new stories are even more captivating to me, I think because they seem more realistic and relatable. Dad went from being that one mischievous kid to someone who finds joy in quiet moments in nature, and life!

Dad's knack for turning ordinary moments into unforgettable adventures is what truly sets him apart. Whether it's a spontaneous road trip to a nearby temple or a scenic spot to watch the sunrise, or simply gathering everyone together to play a new board game. Dad always knows how to infuse excitement into our lives. It's as if our home itself possesses a magical quality, inspiring us to embrace every opportunity for adventure.

Dad makes sure my sisters and I live life to the fullest. He has the most special corner of my mind, allocated to all of him.

Dad shows me how to embrace life with a sense of adventure and an open heart. I’m forever grateful that he is my dad and that he shares these experiences with us. I love him, just as anyone who meets him does. Everyone should know someone who can bring a little adventure into their life. Dad is that person for me. Happiest Father's Day, Papa.

From your dearest, savthi hatpatiyu chokru, maharshi.

Happy father's day.
